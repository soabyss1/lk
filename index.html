A few details about our company
Michaelis Productions is engaged in creating interviews and docummentaries about current topics. The goal is to interview interesting personalities from various fields and learn their views and opinions on current topics. The interviews are then published in the form of podcasts, videos or articles and can be consumed by a wide audience. Through the project, listeners/readers will have the opportunity to gain new perspectives and insights and engage with current issues in an informative and entertaining way.
